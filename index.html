About Me

5+ Years of
Excellence

I am Mohammed Abdul Khaiser, Founder & CEO of HITL PVT LTD, a product-based technology company and a rapidly growing innovation-driven student ecosystem built to bridge the gap between education, industry, and real-world implementation.

My professional journey is deeply rooted in both software and hardware technologies. I have hands-on expertise across Python, Web Development, Data Analytics, Data Science, Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, Embedded Systems, IoT, Robotics, MATLAB, PCB Design, and AutoCAD. This multidisciplinary exposure enables me to design solutions that integrate intelligence, automation, and practical usability.

At HITL PVT LTD, I have built a first-of-its-kind integrated student ecosystem, structured into four strong verticals:

  • HITL Student Community – A unified learning platform offering industry-relevant courses, certified internships, academic and real-time projects, workshops, and continuous career guidance.
  • HITL Solutions – A dedicated innovation and collaboration wing delivering real-time industry projects, product development, research work, and technology solutions.
  • HITL Robotics – Focused on DIY kits, AI training robots, serving robots, and intelligent automation systems.
  • HITL Startup HUB – An incubation and mentoring platform supporting innovators with product development, guidance, and startup growth.

My mission is to create a strong hands-on platform where learners don’t just study concepts but design, build, test, and innovate. I am committed to student career development through structured training, real-world project exposure, and placement-oriented guidance.

I strongly believe that education must evolve from theory-driven learning to experience-driven innovation. Through HITL PVT LTD, I aim to empower students to become skilled professionals, confident innovators, and future leaders.

"Innovation with Education. Skills with Purpose. Careers with Confidence."
